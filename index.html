Decker Library

The Decker Library is the campus library of the Maryland Institute College of Art, located on the first floor and lower level of the Bunting Center. The library contains over 100,000 books and serials, over 5,000 DVDs and access to thousands of electronic journals through subscription databases and hundreds of print journal titles.Additionally, the library has access to over 100,ooo ebooks, over 40,000 digital images and over 1,000 streaming video titles all available through the library catalog.

Decker Library Catalog

While the main focus of the library is the visual arts, the collection also includes a broad and balanced selection of academic and recreational material relating to many subjects.

The Decker Library Catalog is an up-to-date description of all the books, print journals, films/videos, images and other materials located within the Library. It includes circulating and reference materials as well as recently added materials (In Process, or New Book). The search engine includes a feature to select items of interest and to print, email, or save a list of such items. To access the images collections please log-in using your MICA username and password.
